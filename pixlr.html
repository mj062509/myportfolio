Pixlr

Pixlr is an online tool used for editing photos. It allows you to make changes to images, like adjusting colors, cropping, adding filters or removing parts of the picture. Its like a simpler version of Photoshop but you can use it directly in your web browser without downloading anything

From using Pixlr, I learned how easy it is to edit pictures and make them look more professional. I enjoyed playing around with different effects and tools, it gave me a better understanding of how people enhance images for social media or websites. It was fun to be creative and make photos look exactly how I wanted.
